Utilites

Margin:m-{value} , where {value} ranges from 1 to 5. Controls the margin of elements.

Padding: .p-{value}, where {value} ranges from 1 to 5. Controls the padding of elements.

Left/Right/Top/Bottom

For both margin and padding utilities, you can use the class format m{side}-{value} or .p{side}-{value} to apply the utility to a specific side (e.g., left, right, top, bottom).

Replace {side} with l, r, t, or b to indicate the left, right, top, or bottom side respectively.

By using these utility classes, you can easily adjust the spacing and positioning of elements without the need for writing custom CSS rules for each scenario.
